## Legendary Bone Wand

Custom DM item.

### Core Functions

- Functions as an arcane focus for Daisy.
- Allows casting spells without normal material components similarly to most magic focuses, unless the spell or DM says otherwise.
- Allows Daisy to learn any osteomancy spell.

### On-List Osteomancy

If the osteomancy spell is on Daisy's spell list:

- Daisy has advantage on spell attack rolls using that osteomancy spell.
- Enemies have disadvantage on saving throws against that osteomancy spell.
- Wand healing/damage percentage roll is normal.

### Off-List Osteomancy

If the osteomancy spell is not on Daisy's spell list but is learned through the wand:

- Daisy does not get advantage from the wand.
- Enemies do not get disadvantage from the wand.
- The wand healing/damage percentage roll has disadvantage.

#### Arcane Recovery

### Osteomancer Features

#### Brittle Bone Armor

- Bonus action
- Recharges on short or long rest
- Gain **24 temporary HP** for 1 minute or until those temporary HP are lost
- While active: resistance to slashing and piercing damage
- While active: **+4 AC**
- Current active AC if stacking is allowed: **27**

#### Anatomical Expert

- Medicine proficiency
- Add Intelligence modifier to Medicine checks
- If the Medicine check concerns a creature with a skeleton, use double proficiency
- Current values: **Medicine +13 normal / +17 skeleton-related**

#### Bone Puppetry

- Action
- Target one creature with bones within 60 ft.
- Target makes Strength save vs Daisy's spell save DC 18
- On failure, Daisy controls its skeleton until the end of that creature's next turn
- Uses per long rest: **6**, equal to Intelligence modifier
- Does not use a spell slot

#### Skeletal Mastery

- Can cast alter self at will without spell slot and without concentration
- Alter self options allowed: Change Appearance or Natural Weapons
- Can dissolve or restore skeleton as an action
- While boneless:
  - Speed is 10 ft.
  - Can move through spaces as narrow as 5 inches
  - Considered prone
  - Cannot use hands
  - Cannot attack
  - Cannot cast spells
  - Can use bonus action to regenerate bones in hands until end of next turn

## Combat Card

### Before Combat

- Hold Legendary Bone Wand.
- Keep Beelzebub positioned between Daisy and melee enemies if he is deployed.
- Use Guidance outside combat for important checks.
- Use Mage Hand and Minor Illusion for scouting, baiting, distractions, and trap testing.
- Avoid fire whenever possible because Tree gives fire vulnerability.
- Use Brittle Bone Armor before serious combat if timing and DM allow.

### Opening Spell Choices

- Many grounded enemies: **Osseous Impalement**
- One dangerous enemy: **Maiden of Bones** or **Chisel Skull**
- Clustered enemies: **Dread Scarecrow** or **Forest of Dread**
- Need hard battlefield control: **Wall of Force** or **Wall of Bones**
- Need escape/rescue: **Dimension Door**

### Reaction Priority

1. **Counterspell** if an enemy casts a dangerous spell.
2. **Absorb Elements** if taking fire, acid, cold, lightning, or thunder damage.
3. **Shield** if +5 AC can block the hit.
4. **Bone Shield** if the hit is large and Shield will not solve it.

### When Hurt or in Danger

- Use **Dimension Door** to escape with one ally.
- Use **Graveyard Shuffle** if someone needs emergency recovery.
- Use **Fire Shield** before entering fire-heavy danger.
- Use **Brittle Bone Armor** for 24 temp HP and +4 AC if not already active.

### Damage / Control Turns

- Use **Chisel Skull** for single-target burst and paralysis potential.
- Use **Osseous Impalement** for multiple grounded enemies.
- Use **Maiden of Bones** to lock down a single threat.
- Use **Forest of Dread** for area denial.
- Use **Wall of Bones** for 6th-level control/summon pressure.
- Use **Brittle Bone Throw** through the wand when conserving spell slots.
- Use **Eldritch Blast** for long-range force damage.
- Use **Mind Sliver** to set up a later save-based spell.
